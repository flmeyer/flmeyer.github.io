QMA Simulator is a research tool for studying Q-Learning-based Multiple Access (QMA), a resource-efficient, contention-based channel access scheme for the Industrial Internet of Things. It models slot allocation and the routing of broadcast messages for secondary traffic, letting researchers experiment with QMA's parameters over extended runs.

The work builds on peer-reviewed research (ICDCS 2021), implementing and evaluating the published methodology.
