The OMNeT++ Community Summit is the yearly open meeting of the OMNeT++ community, centered on discrete-event simulation of communication networks. The 6th edition was hosted at Hamburg University of Technology on 4–6 September 2019, bringing together 30+ researchers for a hackathon, peer-reviewed presentations, and a keynote on underwater sensor networks.

I was part of the organizing team and built the conference website, which served as the central hub for the call for papers, program, schedule, and registration throughout the event.
